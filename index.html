Support Flood Relief Efforts in Noakhali

Noakhali, a coastal region in Bangladesh, has been severely impacted by recent flooding, leaving thousands of people displaced and vulnerable. Torrential rains and rising river levels have caused widespread damage to homes, crops, and infrastructure. Families are struggling with a lack of clean water, food, and medical supplies.

Support Flood Relief Efforts in Feni

The recent floods in Feni, a district in southeastern Bangladesh, have caused widespread devastation, displacing many families and damaging vital infrastructure. Crops have been destroyed, leading to concerns about food shortages. Many people have been forced to take refuge in temporary shelters, facing health risks due to unsanitary conditions.

Supporting Students Affected by the Quota movement, Bangladesh

The Quota Reform Movement in Bangladesh has been a significant event in recent years, with students from across the country calling for job quota system in public services.While the movement has sparked important discussions about fairness and employment opportunities, many students have faced difficulties academic disruptions and financial strain due to political unrest.
